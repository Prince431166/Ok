AdSense Approval Issues

Your AdSense application was not approved due to policy violations. Based on your Love Compatibility Calculator website, here are the most likely issues and how to fix them:

1. Insufficient Original Content

Your site appears to have limited original content beyond the calculator functionality. AdSense requires substantial, original content that provides value to users.

Action Plan:

  • Add a blog section with relationship advice articles
  • Create detailed explanations of how your calculator works
  • Add personality type descriptions and compatibility explanations
  • Include relationship tips and advice content
View Content Guidelines

2. Thin Privacy Policy Page

Your Privacy Policy needs to be comprehensive and specifically mention how you handle user data, cookies, and third-party advertising.

// Your Privacy Policy should include:
- Data collection methods (forms, analytics, ads)
- Use of cookies and similar technologies
- Third-party disclosure (including AdSense)
- Data protection measures
- User rights and choices
- Contact information for privacy concerns

3. Navigation and User Experience

Your site should have clear navigation and provide a good user experience across all devices.

Improvement Tips:

  • Ensure mobile responsiveness works perfectly
  • Add a clear menu structure with important pages
  • Improve page loading speed
  • Fix any broken links or errors

4. Contact Information

You need a properly accessible Contact page with legitimate contact information.

// Your Contact page should include:
- A working contact form
- Email address
- Physical business address (if applicable)
- Social media links (if available)

5. About Page Quality

Your About page should clearly explain who you are, your expertise, and the purpose of your website.

Enhance Your About Page:

  • Add information about your team or company
  • Explain your mission and values
  • Include author bios with credentials
  • Add professional photos

Implementation Guide

Step 1: Expand Your Content

Add these pages to your site with high-quality, original content:

  • Blog - Regular articles about relationships, love tips, compatibility advice
  • How It Works - Detailed explanation of your compatibility algorithms
  • Relationship Guides - Comprehensive guides on different relationship aspects
  • Testimonials - User experiences with your calculator

Step 2: Enhance Policy Pages

Improve your policy pages to be more comprehensive:

// Privacy Policy should specifically mention:
- Google AdSense and their use of cookies
- How you collect and use data from the calculator
- User consent mechanisms
- Data retention policies
- How users can request data deletion

Step 3: Improve Site Structure

Optimize your website structure for both users and AdSense:

  • Ensure fast loading times (optimize images, minimize code)
  • Implement proper heading structure (H1, H2, H3 tags)
  • Add meta descriptions to all pages
  • Create an XML sitemap and submit to Google Search Console

Step 4: Add More Interactive Elements

Enhance user engagement with additional features:

  • Relationship quiz pages
  • Compatibility comparison tools
  • User account features to save results
  • Social sharing capabilities
